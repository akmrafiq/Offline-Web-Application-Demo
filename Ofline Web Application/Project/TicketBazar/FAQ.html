FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Manage a Booking?

View, print out and e-mail your flight information.
Change your booking date, time and fare class.
Edit your frequent flyer number, contact details, and advance passenger information

How many times can I make use of Manage Booking?

There is no limit on the number of times to use Manage Booking.

What is an electronic ticket?

AirTicketBazars' electronic tickets do away with paper tickets and allow you to book flights conveniently over the internet. After you have completed the online transaction to purchase your tickets, you will be shown a confirmation page and sent a confirmation email, both containing your personal and flight details and a unique reference number. All you need do is print out either one of these and present it along with your photo ID/passport at the check-in desk.

If I book online, how do I get my ticket?

If you book online, you will receive an electronic ticket (also referred to as an ‘eticket’) by email to the address you provided when during the booking process.

How can I use my credit card to book a ticket for someone else?

Just book and pay for the ticket through our online booking engine at AirTicketBazar.com Make sure you enter the cardholder information (name, email address etc) correctly. Note that AirTicketBazar may contact the card holder and require to submit additional information online to confirm the payment, or require to present the credit card at check-in.

Can I cancel my booking online?

No, to cancel your booking and request a refund please send us e-mail to reservation@airticketbazar.com or request directly to AirTicketBazar Sales counter. Please note this is for bookings made directly with AirTicketBazar website or Sales office only. If you booked through a travel agent and haven’t started your journey yet, please contact your travel agent to make any changes. There may be a fee associated with cancelling your booking depending on the fare conditions of your ticket.
